About Me

My Time at KSU

Hello, I am Brakittra Tobtaen! I am currently an undergraduate student here at KSU and am majoring in Information Technology. I am 25 years old and have been a student at KSU since 2018. Wow that seems long doesn't it? Well let me explain how I've been a student here for so long. This all started when I was a freshman enrolling as an undergrad for Computer Science. I unfortunately spent 4 years in a program that just wasn't for me and wasted a bit of time trying to force myself to enjoy CS. Along the way, life happened as well and unfortunately I ran into personal difficulties that made schooling a bit hard. I made the switch to IT about 2 years ago and I'm grateful that I did! I enjoy this major much more and have become more successful in my classes. I'm still grateful for all the wonderful topics I've learned in CS and it has for sure helped me out in my IT journey. I'm expected to graduate in the Spring of 2026 and couldn't be more excited.
